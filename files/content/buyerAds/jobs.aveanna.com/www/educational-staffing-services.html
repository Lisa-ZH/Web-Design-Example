Educational
Staffing Services

When it comes to care, school is always in session.

At Aveanna, we support education in a variety of ways. As a national leader in pediatric care, we have extensive experience working with families, schools, and school systems to meet their specific needs. Our career opportunities range from school nursing to coaching and wellness. Looking to make a difference at school?
Find yourself here!

The benefits of working for Educational Services:

  • Work the schedule of a school district: Monday-Friday, holidays and summers off

  • Immediate placement

  • Competitive compensation

  • Growth opportunities

  • Working with children

A variety of roles. A lifetime of fulfillment.

Nursing and Health Services Technicans
  • One-on-one interventions, such as medication administration and first aid, for students in school clinics.
  • LPNs and RNs provide one-on-one specialty care (vents, trach, G-tubes, medication management)
  • Substitute school nurses
  • Bus transportation and field-trip nursing
  • Assistance with classroom activities as appropriate
  • Ability to work with the school team to support IEPs
  • Complete health tech services
  • CPR/first aid required

Therapy
  • Physical Therapy – Strength and skills improved with clear results in enhanced physical abilities
  • Occupational Therapy – Help with the skills needed for everyday life
  • Speech-Language Pathology – Improving students’ ability to communicate effectively with others

Behavior Therapy
  • Scientific approach helps students achieve their academic and behavioral goals
  • Highly qualified special education professionals and paraprofessionals provide one-on-one behavior interventions 
  • Clinicians consult on behalf of individual students and/or support class- or program-wide initiatives 
  • Ability to provide specialty staffing, such as special education teachers / substitutes / school psychologists
  • Initial and on-going training and support for school staff members

Educational Coaching
  • Scientific approach helps students achieve their academic and behavioral goals
  • Highly qualified special education professionals and paraprofessionals provide one-on-one behavior interventions 
  • Clinicians consult on behalf of individual students and/or support class- or program-wide initiatives 
  • Ability to provide specialty staffing, such as special education teachers / substitutes / school psychologists
  • Initial and on-going training and support for school staff members

Who Benefits from Education Services?

Schools typically utilize our services to support students with special needs, but many of our programs can greatly benefit both typical and atypical students learning at school or from home. Additionally, we believe supporting students means also supporting the adults in their lives. We empower parents, teachers, and staff to assist children in educational and home settings, providing strategies, programs, and resources that help everyone involved.
