Our Medicare Certified therapy is part of the Home Health benefit. We provide therapy in the home, wherever home is, including assisted living facilities.

If you are a therapist looking for a new opportunity, you couldn’t be at a better company than Aveanna. We have a therapy leadership team dedicated to ensuring that therapists have the tools they need to succeed and provide care to their patients.

We have the right job for you.

Whether you are looking for one-on-one care, schedule flexibility, or just the chance to make a real difference, we have the right job for you. Our interdisciplinary team brings seniors the care and support they need to live independently and safely in their homes, where they want to be.
