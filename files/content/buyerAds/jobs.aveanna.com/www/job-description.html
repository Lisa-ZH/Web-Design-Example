Pediatric Day Healthcare Center - Aide / Nursing Assistant

Aveanna Healthcare is hiring compassionate aides and nursing assistants to provide care to medically complex patients in our pediatric day healthcare center. Aveanna Healthcare is one of the largest pediatric home health care companies in the U.S. This is an outstanding opportunity to develop specialized skills and gain career experience in a fun pediatric setting.

Aide Position Details:

  • Provide care and supportive services to children ages birth to 8 years old in a developmentally appropriate center-based setting
  • Assist the nurse in providing daily care according to the physician orders
  • Promote an engaging classroom environment where children can learn and develop
  • Maintain a clean and safe environment
  • Participate as a vital member of our multidisciplinary team

Qualifications for Aide Position:

  • One (1) year of documented experience in the care of infants and toddlers or a medically fragile child
  • Current Healthcare Provider CPR certification (can be obtained during orientation)

Additional Benefits of Aide Position:

  • Available shifts are Monday - Friday Daytime; No nights and no weekends
  • Flexible scheduling for PRN positions
  • 401(k) Savings Plan with Employer Matching
  • Eligible for some healthcare benefits based on employment status
  • Training & Continuous Professional Development as an employee of Aveanna Healthcare

KEYWORDS - PDHC, PPEC, PECC
