Care that works for you

Come join our team today and get a sign-on bonus of $1,500!

Aveanna has nursing jobs that work for you as an RN or LPN. Whether you are looking for one-on-one care, schedule flexibility, or just the chance to make a real difference, we have the right job for you. We bring seniors the care and support they need to live independently and safely in their homes, where they want to be. It’s fulfilling work that will bring you back to the true heart of nursing.

Caring In Action

RN/LPN Responsibilities Include:
  • One-to-one patient care in a home environment.
  • Delivery of specialized nursing care to medically complex, adult patients.
  • Patient assessments and coordination of care.
  • Health promotion, teaching, and training of patients and/or family members.
  • Must be able to work one weekend per month.

RN/LPN Requirements for Success

  • Nurses with med-surg background, Behavioral Health
  • Nurses who are new to Home Health or New Graduates
  • RNs with licenses in good standing in the state where they will practice
  • Nurses with current CPR certification
  • Nurses willing to travel and visit adult patients in their home

Why More Nurses Are Saying YES to Aveanna

  • Health, dental, vision, and life insurance for eligible employees
  • Vacation and sick time available
  • State-of-the-art technology for electronic charting during point of care
  • Flexible scheduling – work days, nights, weekends
  • Virtual interviews, onboarding, and training available
  • 1-on-1 care
  • 24/7 clinical and scheduling support

“I love giving my patients the ability to stay independent and in their homes. This job is so rewarding.”

Our Mission

Aveanna Healthcare is one of the nation’s leading providers of pediatric and adult homecare in the nation. We lead with clinical quality and compassion, delivering care in over 200 locations in 23 states. While we have a national presence, we are very much a local provider in each community we serve. Our stated mission is to revolutionize the way our care is delivered, one patient at a time, and we hope you will help us fulfill that mission by joining the 30,000 nurses who already call Aveanna home.
