Public Health Career Explorer

This tool will help you identify possible career opportunities in public health that match your interests.

Simply click your level of interest for each question. Do not worry about anticipated education or training requirements. Once you complete the questionnaire, you will immediately see your results, which include relevant careers and where to find current opportunities.

Please Note: The Public Health Career Explorer serves as a starting point for your unique career exploration and not as conclusive evidence directing you toward a particular career. It generates a list of health department occupations aligned with your interests, excluding jobs not within health departments.
