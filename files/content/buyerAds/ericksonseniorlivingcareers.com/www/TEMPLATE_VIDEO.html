Hello, Opportunity!

Promising careers start here! Join our team and say hello to great benefits, fantastic work-life balance, and a chance to grow.

We want to hear from you! Tell us why you love working at Erickson Senior Living. Submit your employee experience today and we may use it on our career site.

Getting started

  • Record the video with your phone in a vertical (portrait) position.
  • Ideal smartphone camera settings are 4k or 1080p HD with 24 or 30 fps. Turn on image stabilization.
  • Recommended video length is 1 minute.
  • Find a quiet, well-lit place and face the light. Avoid being directly under overhead lighting.
  • Distance yourself from the background, so you’re not against a wall.
  • Frame yourself from the chest up with your head towards the top of the frame without touching.
  • Introduce yourself using only your first name and job title. Adding your department and facility is optional.

Inspiration

  • Tell us about a day-in-the-life in your role
  • What do you love about working at Erickson Senior Living?
  • Talk about the culture of your department, facility or the company as a whole
  • Give a brief overview of your career path from when you joined us to today
  • Pick one of our values and describe an example that you’ve seen it applied
  • Tell us about one of our benefits that makes the most difference in your life?
  • What would you say to someone who is thinking about joining our system?
  • If you’re a recruiter or hiring leader, describe an open role as if you’re speaking to that ideal candidate, or record a personal invitation to attend an upcoming hiring event.

All submissions may be shared by Erickson Senior Living on social media, in internal communications, or on other platform.
