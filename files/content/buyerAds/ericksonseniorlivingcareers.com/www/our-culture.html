Our Culture

When you work at Erickson Senior Living® or one of our managed communities, you’ll quickly discover that our mission, vision, and values are more than just words on a page. Our culture guides all of our decision-making and sets us apart from other health care and senior living providers. We want this to be the place you build a career for life!

Our Mission

We help people live better lives.

We strive to make life better for employees, residents, and visitors by creating a great experience delivered with genuine care and concern. For team members, that means providing you with:

  • Opportunities to learn and grow your career
  • Exceptional benefits
  • Flexibility
  • A workplace where you feel accepted and celebrated for who you are

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected leader in senior living.

We understand that trust and respect must be earned, by the residents we serve, their families—and our hardworking team members. We learn what matters to you and create action plans to help you thrive, including:

  • Annual employee engagement surveys
  • Yearly performance reviews and regular training to encourage professional development and success
  • Regular, meaningful touch-base conversations with your manager to discuss your work goals, challenges, and interests

Our Values

Seven we-centered statements guide all of our actions and behaviors.

These values are the cornerstone of Erickson Senior Living’s commitment to creating and sustaining a strong culture:

  • We value meaningful relationships.
  • We seek out and celebrate diversity.
  • We are friendly and enthusiastic.
  • We act with integrity.
  • We strive for excellence.
  • We are accountable.
  • We are one team.

Learn More About Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Promises

The three, upsweeping lines in our logo are symbolic of the commitments that we make and keep to our team members and the residents we serve. When you choose to build your career with us, we aspire to provide you with:

  • A vibrant work environment
  • Financial benefits and stability
  • A focus on your health and well-being

Learn More About Our Company Commitments

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Belonging

Seeking out and celebrating diversity is one of our values because it is key to our success. We take purposeful steps to create an inclusive environment where all are welcomed and everyone can be their true and authentic selves, including:

  • Active diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging councils at each community
  • Diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging training for all employees
  • Online learning courses to build inclusive and cultural competence skills
