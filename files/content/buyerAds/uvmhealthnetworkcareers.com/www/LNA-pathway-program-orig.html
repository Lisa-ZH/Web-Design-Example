LNA Pathway Program at University of Vermont Health Network

Get paid to learn while studying to complete the course and receive licensure through the State of Vermont.

Applications are now open for our upcoming classes!

While in class, you will be employed as a Nursing Assistant in training, where you will serve under the supervision of a licensed nurse, and provide indirect nursing care and emotional support for our patients and families.

Upon graduation you will gain firsthand experience working alongside our veteran care providers as you transition to a LNA (VT) or CNA (NY) role.

Classes are held at either University of Vermont Medical Center in Burlington, VT, or Central Vermont Medical Center in Berlin, VT, Hannaford Career Center in Middlebury, VT, the Center for Technology in Essex or the Alice Center in Malone, NY. University of Vermont Health is an integrated academic health system that serves more than one million residents across Vermont and Northern New York. We are working to preserve access to care for our families, friends and communities while also innovating to change the way that care is delivered to focus on wellness as much as illness and to control costs.
