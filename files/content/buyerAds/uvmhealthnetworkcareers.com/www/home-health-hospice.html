Overview

For more than 100 years, we have provided high-quality, compassionate care wherever our community members call home. We support individuals and families at every age and stage of life, from pregnancy and early childhood care to adults with acute and chronic illnesses and those at the end of life.

Home Health and Hospice – Home Health Care is a Journey, Join Us!

Meet some of our dedicated and highly skilled team members, learn more about what makes Home Health and Hospice a truly unique and supportive community, and why we stay. The connections and relationships we build with our patients and community, the opportunity to see your care make a difference, and the ability to have flexibility and autonomy, all while feeling supported.

It is an exciting time to be working in home health. Factors such as the growing aging population, advances in technology, and preferences to age in place are contributing to a strong future for UVM Health – Home Health & Hospice.

Home health’s unique perspective on patient care and proven ability to improve outcomes are being highlighted in health care reform efforts. UVM Health – Home Health & Hospice offers you the opportunity to be part of these initiatives in enhanced care delivery and to become part of a team with a reputation for compassion and quality care.

We are working to preserve access to care for our families, friends and communities while also innovating to change the way that care is delivered to focus on wellness as much as illness and to control costs.
