Workforce Development Pathways at University of Vermont Health

Your future career starts here. Now.

University of Vermont Health is dedicated to providing training and development opportunities to our employees and members of our local communities that help them to gain the skills, education and professional certifications they need to establish rewarding careers in health care.

The programs listed below are some of the ways you can build your skillset and deepen your education through the UVM Health. Many allow participants to work and get paid while they receive training.

Advancement Programs

If you're looking to advance your career in health care, University of Vermont Health offers educational opportunities to help our current and our future employees grow!

UVM Health Pathfinder Program

Pathfinder is a partnership with Community College of Vermont and will support employees who are seeking to enter a UVM Health Pathway Program to begin taking prepaid preparatory, prerequisite and corequisite courses.

Learn More

Licensed Practical Nursing (LPN) Pathway Program

Through our partnership with Vermont State University, our Licensed Practical Nursing Pathway Program allows full-time employees to continue earning a salary while taking classes toward their LPN degree.

Learn More

Registered Nurse (RN) Program

Through our partnership with Vermont State University (VTSU), our RN Pathway Program allows full-time UVM Health LPN employees of our Vermont partners the opportunity to continue earning a salary while studying to complete their Associate of Science in Nursing degree, leading to eligibility for RN licensure.

Learn More

Respiratory Therapy (RT) Program

Through our partnership with Vermont State University and the Community College of Vermont, our RT Pathway Program allows full-time UVM Health employees to continue earning a salary while studying to complete their Associate of Science in Respiratory Therapy degree leading to their Registered Respiratory Therapist (RRT) licensure.

Learn More

UVMMC LNA to RN Pathway Program

Through our partnership with Community College of Vermont (CCV) and Vermont State University (VTSU), UVMMC’s LNA to RN Pathway Program allows employees at UVMMC to continue earning a salary while taking classes toward an advanced healthcare degree.

Learn More

Training Programs – No Experience Required!

At University of Vermont Health, you can join our team at any level of experience! The opportunities below are open to everyone requiring little to no prior experience.

Nursing Assistant Program (LNA/CNA)

Our Nursing Assistant Programs allow participants to earn a salary while studying to complete the course and receive licensure through the State of Vermont or New York.

Learn More

Clinical Assistant (CA) Program

The Clinical Assistant (CA) Program trains qualified applicants to take their place on our patient care team at Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital (CVPH) in New York.

Learn More (link opens in a new window)

Phlebotomist Registered Apprenticeship

Our Phlebotomist Apprenticeship Program is a paid, year-long training program that prepares applicants to become Phlebotomists at the University of Vermont Medical Center.

Learn More (link opens in a new window)

Surgical Technologist Registered Apprenticeship

Coming in 2026: Thanks to funding championed by Senator Peter Welch, this one-year, paid apprenticeship will help build the next generation of Surgical Technologists across Central Vermont, Porter, and UVM Medical Centers.

Learn More (link opens in a new window)

Sterile Processing Technician Registered Apprenticeship

Coming in 2026: Thanks to funding championed by Senator Peter Welch, this one-year, paid apprenticeship will help build the next generation of Sterile Processing Technicians across Central Vermont, Porter, and UVM Medical Centers.

Coming Soon (link opens in a new window)

Education Programs

Not finding the right workforce development program for you? Explore the diverse educational programs below. These unique opportunities will help you gain essential skills to start a rewarding career in health care.

Project SEARCH

Project SEARCH is an innovative, business-led, school to work transition program for young adults with disabilities hosted at University of Vermont Medical Center.

Learn More

School of Radiologic Technology

Our School of Radiologic Technology at Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital (CVPH) is an accredited, 24-month radiographic science program that prepares graduates to sit for the American Registry of Radiologic Technologist (ARRT) examination and earns an associate’s degree from SUNY Empire State College.

Learn More

Paramedic Program

Our Paramedic Education Program is an intensive training program which blends an on-line learning environment, classroom sessions, lab sessions, and clinical experience. It is a 18-month, 1200+ hour program.

Learn More (link opens in a new window)

UVM Health Recognized as a 2024 Frontline Healthcare Worker Champion

University of Vermont Health has been recognized as a national leader for wide-ranging workforce development initiatives by a nationwide health care networking and strategy organization, for an innovative assortment of career training and continuing education programs that serve both employees and members of the community.

Learn More (link opens in a new window)

Growing and Strengthening Our Workforce: Strategic Recruitment & Retention

The national workforce shortage has made a massive impact on health care organizations across the country, and the UVM Health’s region is no exception. Fortunately, UVM Health is taking action to combat the workforce shortage in health care by strategically developing, nurturing and retaining new talent throughout Vermont and northern New York.

We are working to preserve access to care for our families, friends and communities while also innovating to change the way that care is delivered to focus on wellness as much as illness and to control costs.
