Imaging Careers at University of Vermont Health

Career Paths within Imaging at UVM Health are flexible to support our employees’ individual development and professional goals.

Often, our entry level Radiology Technologists are able to expand their skillset into other imaging technologist roles that they find most interesting. At some of our affiliates, employees focus on a single modality (CT, MRI, or Sonography for example), while at other affiliates our teams focus on multi-modality work (conducting Xray, CT scans, mammograms, and more). Regardless of which UVM Health affiliate you choose to join, and your unique professional goals, we’re confident you’ll find a path that supports your growth – whether that be to focus on a single modality or multi-modality.

The exceptional clinical and non-clinical employees at University of Vermont Health are at the forefront of the patient experience. The collegiality of the Radiology team is not only valued by the patients we care for; it’s important to the health and wellbeing of a work life balance.
