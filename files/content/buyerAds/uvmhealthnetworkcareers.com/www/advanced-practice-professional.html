Advance Practice Professional Careers at University of Vermont Health

Leading the Way in Professional Excellence

Bring your knowledge, experience, and clinical expertise to one of the diverse and valued APP roles at the UVM Health. Explore advancing your career through joining our dynamic team of Nurse Practitioners, Physician Assistants, Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists/Certified Anesthesiologist Assistants, and Certified Nurse Midwives among a growing network of medical professionals across Vermont and New York. Learn more about specific opportunities and find the position that is the perfect fit for you while living in an idyllic area of the Northeast surrounded by natural beauty and many outdoor opportunities.

Advanced Practice Talent Areas

  • Certified Nurse Midwife (CNM)

    Certified Nurse Midwives (CNMs) Provide care through all phases of pregnancy and birth, assist patients with their family planning and gynecological care as well as provide basic primary care for people who identify as female.

    View Jobs

  • Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist / Certified Anesthesiologist Assistant (CRNA/CAA)

    Anesthesia APPs at UVM Health are essential members of our care team, taking immense pride in their expertise and collaborative work with anesthesiologists. Together, we ensure safe, high-quality care for every patient during every procedure. Join our anesthesia family, where you’ll be supported by your peers and have the opportunity to build a fulfilling career while enjoying an excellent work-life balance.

    View Jobs

  • Nurse Practitioner (NP)

    As clinicians with at least six years of formal Nursing undergraduate and graduate academic and clinical training, NPs blend clinical expertise in diagnosing and treating health conditions with an added emphasis on disease prevention and health management. NPs are well-equipped to provide high-quality, patient-centered care.

    View Jobs

  • Physician Assistant (PA)

    Physician Assistants play an integral part in the care team, working in collaboration with physicians to provide patient centered comprehensive care. Across numerous divisions and network sites, PAs treat patients throughout the lifespan, using compassionate evidence based practice.

    View Jobs

  • APP Students/Learners Seeking Clinical Experiences

    APP students furthering their academic and clinical preparation by obtaining a graduate degree from a nationally accredited graduate program, can inquire about clinical rotations / experiences in the UVM Health here!

    Learn More

  • Post-graduate APP Transition to Practice Opportunities

    The Transition to Practice Program at CVPH offers Advanced Practice Providers transitioning into Emergency Medicine 12 months of immersive experience as they prepare for collaborative clinical practice. Through dedicated didactic time with content experts, procedure lab held at the state of the art Simulation Center at the Larner College of Medicine, and time spent at the bedside performing US, APPs finish the program with EM competence ready to matriculate into clinical practice.

    Learn More

UVM Health Partner Locations

University of Vermont Health is an umbrella term for our healthcare system family–home to six operating hospitals, over 15,000 employees, and servicing more than one million patients across Vermont and Northern New York. In addition to our six medical center locations, UVM Health is also a home health and hospice provider.
At UVM Health, we do not consider ourselves merely a healthcare system—we are your friends and neighbors. Our communities count on UVM Health and our Partner Locations to provide equitable, affordable, and compassionate healthcare.
