The Employee Referral Program at University of Vermont Health

We’re on an adventure to find exceptional clinical and non-clinical professionals to join our team.

You know what it takes to succeed at University of Vermont Health. And we need you to help us find hidden gems of talent in our communities.

To refer, visit the following page:

ERIN | Employee Referral Portal (erinapp.com) (link opens in a new window)

University of Vermont Health’s Employee Referral Bonus Program offers you up to $5000 award!*

$5,000 — RN, LPN, Respiratory Therapists, Rehab Therapists, Dialysis Technologists, Medical Technologists (includes Lab, Pharmacy and Radiology Techs), Staff Psychologist

$3,000 — LNA/CNA, CPSA, Medical Assistant, Clinical Care Associate, Mental Health Tech., EEG Tech, Surgical Tech, Phlebotomist

$2,000 — all other roles

$250 — all per diem roles

PLUS employees will receive an entry into a quarterly prize drawing for each referral they make.

Simply recommend stand-out candidates to us: friends, family members, former colleagues, acquaintances — anyone you think that would make an outstanding addition to the University of Vermont Health team. Each successfully hired referral you make may lead to a big reward!

*The referral bonus is subject to taxes and will be paid in two installments - 50% at 90 days of referral employment and 50% at 6 months. Referral bonuses are pro-rated 50% for part-time hours.

Participation and Eligibility Guidelines

Many of the new and most promising additions to our team are referred by current employees. The Employee Referral Program was developed to provide an incentive award to a current employee who refers quality candidates that are subsequently hired for designated open positions. Refer friends, family members, former colleagues, acquaintances at ERIN | Employee Referral Portal (erinapp.com) (link opens in a new window)

Eligibility

  • Referral bonuses are distributed in the employees’ paycheck following 90 days (50%) and 6 months (50%) of employment by the referred employee.
  • Both the employee who made the recommendation and the employee who was hired must be employees of UVM Health at the time payouts are disbursed.
  • This program is designed for external hires only and does not apply to internal applicants.
  • Candidates must not have worked at any UVM Health affiliate within the past two years.
  • An employee at any affiliate within the Network can refer to any other affiliate and be eligible.

Exclusions

The following are not eligible to receive a referral award for recommending someone:

  • No leader at the AVP level or above can receive a referral award for recommending any hire.
  • No leader or physician that is in the same SUP Org as the referred new hire can receive the award.
  • No one that is employed in Human Resources can receive a referral award for recommending any hire.
  • Travelers already working at any affiliate cannot be referred for an award.
  • Temporary employees & consultants cannot be referred for an award.

Referral Procedure

To submit referrals visit this page:
ERIN | Employee Referral Portal (erinapp.com) (link opens in a new window)

Questions?

If you have questions, please contact EmployeeReferrals@uvmhealth.org (link opens in a new window).

*Based on market conditions and hiring needs, University of Vermont Health reserves the right to amend, interpret, terminate or suspend this program at any time.
