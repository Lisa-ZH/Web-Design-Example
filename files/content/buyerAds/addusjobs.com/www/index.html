Phishing Alert

We have become aware of a phishing scheme impersonating our company’s brand, falsely claiming employment opportunities that do not exist. As we currently understand it, this deceptive tactic aims to collect personal information from unsuspecting individuals.

We want to emphasize that we do not engage in unsolicited job offers via email or social media chains. If you receive or have received any such communications, we advise you not to respond or provide any personal details. All of our current job opportunities and applications can be found here on this page.

There is nothing more important in life than family, and when you join Addus HomeCare, you’re family.
