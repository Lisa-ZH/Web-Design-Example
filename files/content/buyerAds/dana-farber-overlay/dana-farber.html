We’re seeking a variety of backgrounds and expertise on our teams. Where will you make the greatest impact?

Administration
Administration oversees and drives staffing, learning & development, and operations functions for the Division of Philanthropy’s 300+ staff.
Annual Giving
Annual Giving fundraises through three programs--Direct Marketing, Prospect Management (including Palm Beach fundraising), and Special Programs—to raise $30M+ annually in non-event, unrestricted and restricted gifts of up to $1-$250,000.
Boston Marathon®
Jimmy Fund Walk
The Boston Marathon® Jimmy Fund Walk presented by Hyundai is Dana-Farber Cancer Institute’s signature fundraising event. The Walk features four different routes along the famed Boston Marathon® course as well as the option to participate virtually. The Jimmy Fund Walk has raised more than $175 million since its inception in 1989, and it is the highest fundraising single-day event in the nation.
Campaign
The campaign team is responsible for the strategic planning, management, implementation, and oversight of all activities related to The Dana-Farber Campaign.
Communications
Philanthropy Communications is a dynamic team of mission-driven project managers/writers, graphic designers, and production professionals handling all facets of marketing for events and initiatives serving the Division of Philanthropy. Projects include marketing collateral, publications, proposals, video, animation, special projects, and content for multiple digital platforms.
Corporate Partnerships
Corporate Partnerships secures philanthropic support from companies and corporate foundations through major gifts and grants, creative cause marketing partnerships, and sponsorships for Jimmy Fund events.
