Smile, You're On Camera

Talent Acquisition’s video platform is now available for employees to upload short, informal videos that can be shared internally, with job applicants, on social media or be featured on our career website. Your informative video will give potential applicants and new candidates a glimpse into what it’s like to lead with us!

We want to hear from you! Tell us why you love working at Houston Methodist Hospital. Submit your employee experience today and we may use it on our career site.

Getting started

  • Find a quiet, well-lit place and face the light. Avoid being directly under overhead lighting.
  • Distance yourself from the background, so you're not against a wall.
  • Ideal smartphone camera settings are 4k or 1080p HD with 24 or 30 fps. Turn on image stabilization.
  • Record the video with your phone in a horizontal (landscape) position.
  • Introduce yourself using only your first name and job title. Adding your department and hospital is optional.
  • Recommended video length is 30 seconds - 2 minutes
  • After recording, edit the video by cropping it using a 1:1 ratio. This helps frame your head and shoulders and keeps our videos looking consistent.
  • Feel free to shorten the video in edit mode to highlight the best clip. Now you’re ready to upload your video!

Inspiration

  • Tell us about a day-in-the-life in your role.
  • What do you love about working at Houston Methodist?
  • Talk about the culture of your department, hospital or the system as a whole.
  • Give a brief overview of your career path from when you joined Houston Methodist to today.
  • Pick one of the iCARE values and describe an example that you've seen it applied.
  • Which Houston Methodist benefit makes the most difference in your life?
  • What would you say to someone who is thinking about joining Houston Methodist?
  • If you're a recruiter or hiring leader, describe an open role as if you're speaking to that ideal candidate. Or record a personal invitation to apply, interview or attend an upcoming hiring event.

All submissions may be shared by Houston Methodist Hospital on social media, in internal communications, or on other platform.
