Summer Operations Internship Program
Hospital Administration

The Houston Methodist Summer Operations Internship program is an innovative, collaborative, 10-week paid program that allows interns to gain unparalleled exposure to the operations of a premier academic medical center.

Designed for graduate students interested in a career in hospital administration/operations

Each intern will work directly with a high-level director on a variety of process improvement, operational, and financial projects in the Texas Medical Center main campus. Prior interns have had the opportunity to work in departments such as: Cath Lab, Operating Room, IT, Finance, Global Services, Case Management, Transplant Center, Pharmacy, Outpatient Center, Supply Chain Management, and many more.

In addition to completing their assigned projects, Summer Operations Interns actively engage in various learning and networking opportunities with executives and leaders across the hospital

Summer Operations Interns expand their understanding of hospital operations and leadership through the following:

  • Weekly executive roundtable sessions with Houston Methodist executives
  • Department deep dive presentations by operational leaders
  • Hospital tours in clinical, support services, and innovation areas
  • Professional development activities such as resume and interview workshops
  • “ICARE in Action” community service volunteer day
  • Communication with an assigned mentor
  • Networking with hospital leaders, former interns, and others

Application Requirements:

We are now accepting applications for our 2026 Internship Program. The application period will run from November 3, 2025 to January 30, 2026. Application components include your resume and a personal statement of interest explaining why you are applying to Houston Methodist and what you expect to gain from your internship experience (500 words or less). Applications are due by 11:59PM CST on January 30, 2026.

Please submit all materials in one combined PDF to opsinternship@houstonmethodist.org during the application period (November 3, 2025 to January 30, 2026).

We will be holding a Virtual Information Session on Tuesday, January 13, 2026 at 5:00PM CST. This will be a great time to learn about the program structure, potential projects and professional development opportunities that will be offered in the summer. There will be an open Q&A session, so be sure to bring your questions! Please register for the Virtual Information Session using this form: https://forms.office.com/r/1uDGEfTr7t.

The 2026 Summer Operations Internship Program will run from June 1 to August 7.

Please note that this internship is for graduate students interested in hospital administration. Undergraduate students, high school students, and/or students interested in clinical or research internships should refer to our Frequently Asked Questions section for other summer opportunities at Houston Methodist.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to the Summer Operations Internship Program?

To apply, please submit your resume and a personal statement of interest explaining why you are applying to Houston Methodist and what you expect to gain from your internship experience (500 words or less) in one combined PDF to opsinternship@houstonmethodist.org. All applications must be submitted by 11:59PM CST on January 30, 2026.

Who is eligible to apply to the Summer Operations Internship Program?

To be eligible for the Summer Operations Internship Program, applicants must be enrolled in an MHA, MBA, MPH, or other healthcare-related graduate level degree program, or have graduated with one of the aforementioned degrees in the last 2 years.

Can undergraduate students, high school students, and/or students interested in clinical internships apply?

No, this internship is for graduate students interested in hospital administration. Below are the contacts for other summer opportunities at Houston Methodist:

  • Nursing internship: contact shamlin@houstonmethodist.org
  • Medical residency or internship: visit https://www.houstonmethodist.org/education/medical/graduate-medical-education/
  • Research internships: visit https://www.houstonmethodist.org/education/medical-graduate-student/summer-internship-program/
  • Volunteer services: visit https://www.houstonmethodist.org/giving/volunteer/

Is this a paid internship?

Yes. Beginning in 2024, Summer Operations Interns will be paid a stipend during their 10-week internship program.
