Southcoast Health Careers

Southcoast Health is a not-for-profit, charitable health system with multiple hospitals, clinics and facilities throughout Southeastern Massachusetts and Rhode Island. Nestled within local communities, Southcoast Health provides inclusive, ethical workplaces where our highly skilled caregivers offer world-class, comprehensive healthcare close to home. Join Southcoast Health, where your future is as promising as the care we provide. Our commitment to each other, our patients and our community is more than a mission - it’s our way of life, and you’ll be at the heart of it.
