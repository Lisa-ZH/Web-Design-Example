Our Culture. Your Career.

Here you’ll have the opportunity to collaborate with dedicated professionals from diverse backgrounds—from experienced faculty to staff members who are experts in their respective fields. This creates a dynamic environment that helps define our mission, our work, and our global leadership...and every extraordinary career we build.

Mission Driven. Focused on Excellence.

Our goal is simple:
To provide adult learners with the flexibility, creativity and excellence-driven degree programs to change their lives for the better.

  • Our Values

  • Celebrate Diversity

    Our welcoming of diverse perspectives and ideas differentiates us and drives innovation

  • Optimize Agility

    Curiosity and adaptability—informed and guided by data—drive continuous improvement and transformation

  • Reach Beyond

    Courage and willingness to challenge boundaries lead to transformative solutions, for our institution and our learners alike

  • Embrace Collaboration

    Teamwork, effective communication, and clarity of purpose drive success

Building on a proud tradition of delivering higher education to our military worldwide, we’re leveraging the University of Maryland’s academic strength to change the face of online education again. It’s a powerful and innovative model, and one that means exciting and meaningful work for people who want to positively impact real lives across the globe.

We're giving talented people an environment where they can create solutions that fit their ambitions and their lives.

By integrating a passionate, entrepreneurial spirit with work/life balance, innovative technologies and a forward-focused mindset, we’re building a remarkable team that’s actively transforming the face of online higher education. Again.
