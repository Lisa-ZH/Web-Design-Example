As a global, online university and technology pioneer, UMGC offers exceptional careers to professionals in a wide range of fields.

The university offers an array of career options, along with ample opportunities for growth and advancement. Explore the broad range of faculty and staff positions and become a member of the UMGC community.

Advisors

As part of our world-class enrollment management team, you’ll enjoy:
  • An extraordinary opportunity for new graduates
  • Extensive training and mentoring program
  • Strong foundational salary and benefits package, including tuition remission
  • A focus on quality — and on playing an active role in helping people build a better future

Professional Support & Services

Regardless of your area of interest, we're creating a new standard for what you should expect from your career. As a global, online university and technology pioneer, UMGC offers exceptional career opportunities in a wide range of fields, including Marketing, Financial Aid, Finance, Administration and much more, all featuring:
  • Collaborative, cross-functional teams comprised of experts from diverse backgrounds
  • Opportunities to see your work implemented on a global scale
  • Excellent benefits, including tuition remission, and a variety of retirement options
