My Career Success Center

Exploration & Mentoring

Navigating Your Journey — Your Career, Your Way

Lee Health provides tools and resources to help you explore career paths, define your goals, and take confident next steps.

  • Career Cafés to explore our expansive healthcare career opportunities
  • Career Lee Pathway Program to develop your personalized career journey
  • Job shadows to learn more about new opportunities
  • Career guidance to assist with the next steps
  • Mentorship opportunities to expand your network

Earn While You Learn

Train today, thrive tomorrow

Start your journey! With our paid training programs, you can build skills, earn money, and take steps towards a successful career.

Lee Health currently offers these paid training programs:

  • Medical Assistant
  • Pharmacy Technician
  • Instrument Technician
  • Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)
  • Child Development Associate (CDA)
  • Student Nurse Apprentice (SNAP)

Education Assistance

Fuel Your Future — Pathways to Possibility

At Lee health, team members are empowered to pursue education without financial barriers.

  • Generous education assistance available after 90 days of employment
  • Lee Health sponsored grant and training opportunities
  • Loan forgiveness and reimbursement programs - we are a qualified employer! (HRSA, FRAME, PSLF)
  • Community funded scholarship awards

Training & Development

Advancing Care Through Learning — Empower Your Growth

Your learning never stops. At Lee Health, we offer tools, programs, and resources that support your personal and professional development.

  • Learn@Lee Health online learning platform that offers 24/7 access to personal and professional development
  • Accredited residency and fellowship programs that focus on developing the next generation of healthcare professionals
  • Technology Education to enrich your IT knowledge
  • Digital Badges to earn micro credentials
  • Training Opportunities to expand within your current role

Certifications & Continuing ED

Build Your Credentials & Unlock Your Professional Potential

Enhance your expertise through professional development courses, and industry recognized certifications.

  • Our development programs foster Lee Health’s commitment to internal promotions and “Growing our Own”
  • Lee Health supports advancement and encourages team members to earn professional certifications
  • Clinical ladders support our clinician’s growth and allow them to be recognized for advancing their profession.
  • Accredited by the Florida Medical Association (FMA) to provide Continuing Medical Education (CME)
  • Nursing Grand Rounds encourages team learning by sharing insights, presenting case studies, and highlighting best practices

Growth & Mobility

Move Forward with Confidence

Your career path is unique, and Lee Health is here to support you every step of the way, whether you’re advancing your career, exploring different roles, or nearing retirement.

  • Career guidance to assist you throughout the journey
  • Endless opportunities for advancement across the continuum of care
  • Innovative and evidence-based education offered through highly skilled leadership and organizational development specialists and clinical educators
  • The Life Option program helps you plan for your future even as you consider retirement
  • The Magnet Journey of Excellence reflects our commitment to developing our staff, the medical community, and our patients
  • The Sterling Award reflects our collective effort and our shared commitment to continuous improvement and elevating performance
