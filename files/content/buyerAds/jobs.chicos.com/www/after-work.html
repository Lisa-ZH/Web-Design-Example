Fort Myers offers an exceptional quality of life, an active lifestyle, beautiful beaches, year-round sunshine, and thriving arts and culture scene.

WELCOME to ISLAND LIFE

With fantastic weather and beautiful surroundings, residents like to be outside and on the move. From pristine beaches and lush parks to outdoor restaurants and events, there is always something to do. Whether you are an adventure seeker that wants to go deep-sea fishing or kayak the Caloosahatchee River, or someone more into museums and opera, Fort Myers has year-round attractions to keep your body and your mind active. Enjoy easy access to wildlife, hiking, biking and paddling at various national, state and local parks.

SPORTS in FORT MYERS

Fort Myers is also home for Spring Training. Residents can get up close as they watch the Boston Red Sox at JetBlue Stadium and the Minnesota Twins at Hammond Stadium take on other major league teams of the Grapefruit League. The Florida Everblades is a minor league hockey team affiliated with the Carolina Hurricanes.
