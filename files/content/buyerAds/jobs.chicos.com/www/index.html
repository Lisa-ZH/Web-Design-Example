Our passion for fashion and desire to inspire confidence and joy have been guiding the creation of our women’s clothing, intimates, and accessories for more than 40 years. Our portfolio consists of three brands: Chico’s, WHBM, and Soma found in over 1,000 stores throughout the United States and online. Our commitment and dedication come from our loyal customers and devoted Associates, who are equally inspired by our values. If you share our passion for fashion, we invite you to join us.
