At Chico’s FAS, we value the diverse experiences, perspectives and backgrounds of our Associates and customers. We are committed to an inclusive environment that celebrates our individuality, influences our culture, and innovates the way we work.

Everyone at Chico’s FAS has a voice and is encouraged to be their true selves. Throughout the year, we celebrate the diversity of our Associates through various campaigns, events, and activities.

POWER in the NUMBERS

  • 12 Inclusion and diversity inspired holiday and observance events and resources calendars

  • 12+ Communications and internal site pages with curated content recognizing the importance of Juneteenth and Black History, Asian American Pacific Islander Heritage, Hispanic Heritage and more

  • 100% of new hires introduced to our I&D programs & inclusive culture

Associate Network Groups

At our Corporate Campus, one way we celebrate diversity is through social groups that connect Associates with common interests and shared values.

  • I&D Council
  • Book Club
  • Community Service
  • Retail Leadership
  • Health & Fitness
  • Foodies
  • Pet Lovers

FREE TO BE ME

Our “Free to Be Me” campaign celebrates our individuality and inclusivity through shared stories, experiences and appreciation. We encourage each of our Associates to be themselves, to bring their true selves to work every day and to celebrate each person’s uniqueness.
