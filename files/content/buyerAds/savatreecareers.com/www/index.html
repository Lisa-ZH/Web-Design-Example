Share In Our Growth

SavATree was created 45 years ago to save huge populations of trees threatened by a gypsy moth invasion. Now nationwide, we are home to teams of people who came here with very different backgrounds and have built extraordinary careers by providing the very best care available for trees, shrubs and turf.

View Job Opportunities Nationwide

SavATree currently provides environmentally sensible tree care and lawn care service to clients in many regions of the country. Click on the interactive map to see our office locations and learn more about opportunities close to where you reside!

We Invest in You

  • Growth for the Curious at Every Level.

    When you work here, you thrive here, whether in your current role or on to the next. People with two-year, four-year, or high school degrees can find a rewarding career path, along with vigorous training and support programs.

  • Empowering People Is in Our Nature.

    We teach our people everything we can, including how to make the right decisions and trust their own judgment. We have built a culture of trust and accountability brings out the very best in people.

  • Great People Are Supporting You.

    Your home branch is also your home, where leaders and colleagues provide guidance and support. You’ll consult on landscape issues with experts in arboriculture, horticulture, and agronomics. Marketing makes sure opportunities are there to pursue.

Summer Intern Program

Bring your passion, ambition, and desire to do things differently. You’ll work with collaborative colleagues and mentors who will challenge you and help you develop important career skills that will allow you to thrive.

SavATree’s internship program offers experience in all of our service lines so students and grads get real world experience, and an opportunity to make an impact, too.

We recruit students pursuing related fields and each of our branches is encouraged to sponsor an Intern that is coordinated through our Talent Team. The SavATree internship is different from a “summer work” because it provides exposure to our service lines, sales, and administrative operations. Come show us what you’ve got.
