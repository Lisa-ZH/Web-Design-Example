ABOUT ACCOUNTING / FINANCE JOBS AT UNFI

Accounting and Finance Jobs with UNFI

Apply online now for Finance and Accounting Jobs with UNFI. At UNFI, we know that our people are our greatest assets, and it has been that way since we first opened our doors in 1924. We are committed to the philosophy of providing our employees with internal training, career development opportunities, and benefits that meet their needs. Our ability to grow our business over time by consistently meeting our customers’ precise specifications is a testament to our past employees, and the employees who serve our customers today.

Accounting and Finance Jobs with UNFI can include:

Cost Accountant - Team members in these accounting jobs oversee the material flow, including all inventory activity at selected facilities.

Business Unit Finance Manager, ETP - Team members in these finance jobs are responsible for financial and strategic planning, business performance, process improvement, and investment analysis.

Manufacturing Staff Accountant - Team members in these accounting jobs perform a variety of manufacturing related accounting activities including preparing journal entries and balance sheets, as well as performing monthly and yearly audits of efficiency, spending, costs, and trends.
