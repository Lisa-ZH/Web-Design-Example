Overview

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS

  • On a daily basis, keeps work area clean including equipment, floors, boxes, trash.
  • On a daily basis keeps accurate records and notes on production sheets.
  • On a daily basis, follow test procedures on finished goods and record results that may include; melt flow, specific gravity, hardness (test stand and handheld), etc.
  • On a daily basis, adhere to proper and safe fork truck operation.
  • On a daily basis, communicate with shift leaders about processing problems and be able to troubleshoot and make necessary/permitted adjustments to fix them as well as document and communicate adjustments according to protocol.
  • On a daily basis, be able to take apart, clean, and reassemble equipment.
  • On a daily basis, assist line mixer and other line operators when needed.
  • Additional duties as required/requested.
  • MUST be able to work 3 different shifts (7am-3pm, 3pm-11pm, 11pm-7am).

SKILLS

  • Safe and responsible operation of manufacturing equipment
  • Basic math skills
  • Ability to troubleshoot processing problems
  • Ability to follow and understand set-up sheets
  • Strong communication skills (both verbal and written)
  • Attention to quality and detail

EXPERIENCE

  • Prior manufacturing experience preferred
  • Forklift experience a plus

EDUCATION

  • High School diploma or GED
