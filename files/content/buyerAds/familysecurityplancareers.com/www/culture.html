YOUR SUCCESS IS OUR SUCCESS

We care about our employees. We are proud to offer a strong work life balance, a supportive environment, and a place where our employees can thrive.

Employee Value Proposition

  • Quality of Life — A healthy balance between work and life outside of work.

    • We put families first and understand the need for flexibility.
    • We offer wellness programs to help our employees stay healthy in mind, body, and spirit.
    • We work hard yet find time to have fun together.
    • We recognize and celebrate our individual and team accomplishments.

  • EVERY LIFE MATTERS — GOING ABOVE & BEYOND

    • We thrive on helping others; our customers, our communities, and each other.
    • We believe in the unending pursuit of protecting families and educating our customers on what matters most.
    • Every person working here is valued, heard, and respected.

  • ACHIEVING SUCCESS — BEING PROFESSIONALS WITH INTEGRITY

    • Hard work does not go unnoticed, and victories are recognized and celebrated.
    • We believe in helping each other through individual and team accountability.
    • Whether you are just starting out or have put in decades of service, you have the ability to grow within the FSP and follow the path you want and deserve.

  • Celebrating and Connecting with One Another

    • We have a family-oriented environment where we rally together in support of one another in good times and in bad.
    • We build strong relationships and actively engage with all departments and regions.
    • We collaborate and share knowledge across the company.
    • We regularly engage in helping others and giving back to our communities.

COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Giving back to the community is a core value to The Family Security Plan®. As a company, we are committed to service as a way for us to engage with the community and one another. Some initiatives and causes we support include:

  • Confetti Foundation
  • W.H.E.A.T (West Haven Emergency Assistance Taskforce)
  • American Cancer Society - Making Strides Against Breast Cancer
  • March of Dimes – Blue Jeans for Babies
  • Connecticut Food Bank
  • Columbus House Inc.
  • Do Good Live Well
  • Good Secrets and Bad Secrets
  • Earth to Mars STEM Program
  • Kaiser’s Room
  • Milford Food 2 Kids
  • The Rafael Hernandez School
  • Bridgeport Child Advocacy Coalition (BCAC)
  • Big Brothers Big Sisters of Miami
  • And many more
