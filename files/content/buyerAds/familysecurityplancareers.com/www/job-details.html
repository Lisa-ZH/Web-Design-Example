Job Description

At The Family Security Plan, our focus is on the employee and their growth. From your first day with us, you’ll be joining a company that values everyone’s opinion, rewards and recognizes exemplary work, and prioritizes diversity, equity, and inclusion. You will be part of a culture that cares about you and also loves to have fun! We make a difference in people's lives every day. There's no better career than finding something you love and getting paid for it. Are you our next dynamic employee?

This supervisory position is responsible for the day to day floor activity, coaching, training, performance reviews for a staff of 20+ employees in the department. This position insures that the employees are on target for the goals set for the department. Customer Connect supports Customer Care, CAT, MBC, and Hybrid. Responsible for overseeing the campaigns that Customer Connect is responsible for insuring that PFP members remain protected.

  • Supervise staff of 20+ employees
  • Approve PTO and balance schedule and staffing.
  • Ensure everyone is clocked in/out daily; adjust time accordingly.
  • Answer any/all questions and concerns.
  • Coordinate and lead monthly departmental meetings with the AVP-Manager.
  • Trouble shoot computer/telephone issues (this occurs at least 2 times a day, some days up to 5 times)
  • Work with individuals first to help resolve any issue. i.e.: Task Manager, reboot before contacting IT.
  • Ensure IT ticket is placed for resolution if necessary.
  • Follow-up with individual once IT resolves issue.
  • QA reviews (Monthly):
    • Receive QA’s from QA Evaluator, listen and review. Send to the individual with comments and ask that they listen to themselves and self-assess to determine what they can improve on. Include as a topic in their quarterly development review meetings.
  • Perform Quarterly Reviews.
  • Meet to discuss and review quarterly averages of all metrics; needs or wants. Keep the CCS informed of their performance, contributions and areas of opportunity.
  • Handle personal/personnel matters.
  • Conduct Annual Performance Assessments.
  • Interview and onboard new hires.
  • Monitor floor activity.
  • Oversee department coaching program (team leads also report to Department Manager).
  • Handle member escalations as needed/requested from CCS’s & MBC Agents, and bring to resolution.

Qualifications

  • Minimum of 5 years’ experience in a call center environment.
  • Supervisory experience preferred.
  • Associate’s Degree required.
  • Bachelor’s Degree preferred.
  • Strong organizational skills.
  • Good business acumen.
  • Demonstrates strong leadership skills.
  • Training and coaching experience required.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Drive for results.
  • Sense of urgency.
  • Ability to motivate and inspire others.
