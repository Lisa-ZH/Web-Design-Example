Resumes Tips

  • Refine your selling message (a brief description of what you are looking for and what you offer)
  • Position the most relevant points first, where they are most likely to be read
  • Call attention to your achievements by including results and supporting data
  • Your main selling points should be clear and easy to review
  • Update your resume frequently to ensure your most recent accomplishments are captured
